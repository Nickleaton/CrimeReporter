A man who openly dealt drugs from the back of a parked car in Cambridge has been jailed.
A member of the public contacted police on the afternoon of 30 September 2023 after witnessing Kiama Guy dealing drugs on Regent Terrace.
The 27-year-old, of no fixed address, was handing over small packets to multiple people approaching his white Audi from a holdall in the boot of the car in return for cash.
When police arrived, Guy ran from the vehicle. He later returned to the car topless and without the holdall and claimed he had been eating at a nearby restaurant. Guy was arrested and the bag containing class A and B drugs were seized.
At Cambridge Crown Court on Thursday (29 January), Guy was sentenced to two years in prison after pleading guilty to possession with intent to supply cannabis, ketamine, MDMA and Psilocin
Detective Constable Abbie Ellis, who investigated, said: “This was blatant drug dealing in a busy public area, witnessed by members of the community. Their quick reporting allowed us to respond quickly and secure vital evidence. We will continue to target those who choose to bring drugs and associated harm into our communities.”
