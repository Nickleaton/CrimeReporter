A 25-year-old drug dealer, Curtis Clark, has been ordered to repay nearly £400,000 or face an additional four-year jail term. The Proceeds of Crime Act hearing at Leeds Crown Court mandated he repay £393,363.77 within three months. Clark, linked to an organized crime group, was previously sentenced to 65 months for drug supply and money laundering.
