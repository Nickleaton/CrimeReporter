Hitchin County Lines drug dealer jailed

text

A dealer who was part of a drug gang operating in Hatfield has been jailed for three years.
Kamarley Jones-Ebanks
Following an investigation by Hertfordshire Constabulary’s County Lines Investigation Unit, enquiries were carried out at an address in Hitchin in July 2025. Kamarley Jones-Ebanks, aged 23, formerly of Highover Way, Hitchin, was arrested in connection with drug offences in Hatfield.
Jones-Ebanks appeared at St Albans Crown Court on Thursday 29 January and was sentenced to three years in prison after pleading guilty to being concerned in the offer to supply Class A (crack cocaine).
Detective Sergeant Chris Cowell, from the Serious and Organised Crime Command, said: “This operation is part of a long running investigation into a drug line operating in Hatfield supplying crack cocaine, of which Jones-Ebanks was a key member.
“These drug lines are run by organised crime groups who use violence and commit a whole range of crimes that pose a real threat to the community. Our investigations depend on information from members of the public to help us crack down on drug-related crime. Any information you can provide, no matter how small it may seem, could help us to identify and apprehend drug dealers.”
