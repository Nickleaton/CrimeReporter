We have charged a 15-year-old boy with murder following the death of 16-year-old Shayne Hambakachere.
We have charged a 15-year-old boy with murder following the death of 16-year-old Shayne Hambakachere.
Shayne, a pupil at Hardenhuish School, sadly died after being found seriously injured shortly before 4pm in London Road, Chippenham on Saturday (31/01).
The 15-year-old, a white British boy, was arrested shortly afterwards on suspicion of grievous bodily harm with intent.
He was subsequently re-arrested on suspicion of murder and has today been charged with that offence.
The teenager, who remains in custody and cannot be named for legal reasons, is due to appear before Swindon Magistrates tomorrow (04/02).
Wiltshire Police would like to offer our sincere condolences to Shayne’s family and friends at this incredibly difficult time.
We would also like to thank the community in Chippenham for their ongoing support and recognise the concern that this will have had for everyone in the area.
Detectives continue to appeal for information relating to this ongoing murder investigation.
A Major Incident Public Portal has been set up to allow anyone to directly report information to the investigation team.
You can also leave information anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
