An 18-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder following the stabbing of a university student in Leicester city centre. The incident occurred shortly after 5pm on Tuesday near De Montfort University on Oxford Street, close to Bonners Lane. The victim, a student in his 20s, was stabbed during an altercation and was taken to Leicester Royal Infirmary, where he later died.
